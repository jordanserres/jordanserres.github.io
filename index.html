Research:

Main research interests:

  • Stein's method, spectral inequalities, Poincaré inequalities, Markov semigroup
  • Geometry of metric measure spaces satisfying a Curvature-Dimension condition
  • Isoperimetric inequalities, Geometry in Gauss space.
  • Stability of functional inequalities, Renormalization group
