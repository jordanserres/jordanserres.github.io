Recherche:

Main research interests:

  • Geometry of convex bodies, Brunn-Minkowski theory, extremal problems, volumes of sections and projections.
  • Convolution inequalities, Prékopa-Leindler type inequalities, applications of mass transport and semigroup interpolation.
  • Geometry in Gauss space.
  • Isoperimetric inequalities for measures, functional versions, comparison theorems for products.
  • Concentration of measure, Sobolev inequalities, hypercontractivity, Hardy type criteria.
  • Entropy produced by convolution, rate of convergence in the entropic central limit theorem.
  • Probabilistic study of optimization problems.
